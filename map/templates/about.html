WHY IS OPEN DEMOCRACY GROUNDBREAKING?

We believe in Democracy as a system that guarantees the most freedom to mankind. We also acknowledge world democracies are in crisis. Citizens from every corner of planet Earth are demanding changes, a better society requires an open democracy.
We understand that it is in politics where society’s big decisions are taken, and we’ve been there, in the campaigns front-lines competing to win an election. Politics is highly competitive and access to data is a critical component to thrive.
We’ve created a new generation of tools that give data power to people and not just to the few that so long have been able to afford it. With the right software our democratic systems can better represent the wind of change blowing from people and nature.
