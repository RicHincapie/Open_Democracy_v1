Open Democracy

We are the first free platform to interactively visualize complex data for electoral purposes and designed to facilitate data analysis. Through data we empower citizens, organized groups and parties to be more competitive and make our democracy better.

HOW ARE WE CHANGING POLITICS?

Select a candidate


Unleash Open Democracy’s data visualization capacities to understand the 2019 electoral process results in Cali, Colombia. Very soon you will find a lot more datasets of other elections and cities.

Navigate the polling places


Hover the different points to get information about the total amount of ballots in favor of the candidate, the polling place name and more. Points size and colour hint you about how good the result was.

Aggregate data by Comuna


Use this powerful feature to understand how Comunas' results impacted overall elections and better plan where you should focus the next political campaign efforts.

Enable and disable the layers


Data is more valuable when you put it in context. Open Democracy’s layer features put together complex information for you to have a better understanding of the electoral process.
